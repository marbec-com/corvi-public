Project Corvi

Studying Simplified

Corvi is a social learning app which works similar to an index-card box. You can create boxes to which questions like vocabulary, formulae, definitions or similar can be added. You get tested on those questions in regular intervals, depending on your learning progress. Corvi makes sure, that new questions are learned stepwise to keep daily learning sessions short. At the same time it takes care that previously learned questions stay in memory.
Manage
  • Summarize information on cards
  • Organize cards in boxes
Learn
  • Get tested on your cards
  • Repeat learned cards
Share
  • Share your boxes with others
  • Discover new boxes on exciting topics
